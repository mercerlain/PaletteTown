You may wonder at my motives.

I will try to make everything clear, in time. I want you to feel stable footing while we are traveling together.

But it must be remembered that clarity is a product of focal distance. That is to say…different things become clear through slight alterations to the lens or the looker. Some things go out of focus as others become sharp.

There is no absolute clarity. There is only…mood. I think mood is the closest I can get.

It is my sincere hope that, in time, we shall learn to navigate each other’s moods, so that our traveling together will ring out into the grander world with tones of harmony.
